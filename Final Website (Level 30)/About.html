About Me

Hello, my name is Ryan Eastwood and I am an artist based in Greensboro, NC.

I'm currently studying at UNCG pursuing a BFA in New Media & Design

I mainly focus in hand-drawn mediums, however I enjoy dabbling in digital and photography based work from time to time as well.

Art has always held a special place in my art as a way to express myself visually rather than simply through speech.

I've studied and been inspired by the works of Andy Warhol, Picasso, Jeff Koons and countless others.

I'm constantly looking for new inspirations and to push myself further as an artist by experimenting with new things all the time.
